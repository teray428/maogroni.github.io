ABOUT US!

"From our roots to your table, the sweetness of Filipino culture."

Sugbo's Sweet Treats is a Cebu-based project that promotes Filipino Kakanin by sharing its rich flavors and cultural roots through a modern website.

We aim to keep this tradition alive and accessible for all, one sweet bite at a time
